About Me

I am an economics doctoral student at Boston University. My research interests concern both labor economics and corporate finance. I am also interested in econometrics, in which I focus more on practical genres. Prior to entering Boston University, I earned my Bachelor’s degree in economics from Zhejiang University in China. Post-graduation, I worked as a Research Assistant with the National Bureau of Economic Research. Currently, I have research projects concerning variable Chinese firm dynamics and the policy implications that result.
